Transformer Architecture

First introduced in Google's 2017 paper "Attention Is All You Need", this neural network architecture enables parallel processing of text sequences through self-attention mechanisms. Unlike traditional RNNs, transformers can analyze entire sentences simultaneously, making them exponentially more efficient.
